Render Environment Checklist

  • SUPABASE_URL, SUPABASE_ANON_KEY, and SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY are set.
  • At least one shared provider key is set: SHARED_GEMINI_API_KEY, SHARED_GROQ_API_KEY, or SHARED_OPENROUTER_API_KEY.
  • SHARED_AI_DAILY_REQUEST_LIMIT and SHARED_AI_DAILY_TOKEN_LIMIT are set.
  • DAILY_NEWS_CRON_SECRET is a long random value, not a public/simple word.
  • TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN and TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID are set for free daily updates.
  • CODER_EXECUTION_MODE is draft_only unless you intentionally want server execution.
